## Decision

Use **Penumbra** as the working brand across the operating record so the company
has a coherent identity now. We explicitly flag this as **provisional**.

## Why "Penumbra"

The penumbra is the graded region between full light and full shadow — a precise
metaphor for inclusive design: serve the full spectrum of human ability, not just
the brightly-lit center. It pairs with the tagline "inclusive by default" and
avoids sounding like a compliance vendor.

## Why this is escalated, not decided

Naming carries **trademark/legal risk**, which the mandate reserves to the
Chairman. A notable publicly-traded company (Penumbra, Inc., medical devices) uses
the name in an unrelated class. A design-services mark is likely in a different
Nice class, but **clearance is a legal determination, not ours to make.**

## What we need from the Chairman

- Approve "Penumbra," or choose from alternates (e.g. *Liminal*, *Even*), or
  propose another.
- Authorize trademark search and clearance in the relevant classes/jurisdictions.

Until then, the name is a single configuration value and one decision record —
trivially changed.
