## Decision

- **Two-way doors (reversible):** any founder-agent decides after seeking advice;
  log and move on.
- **One-way doors (hard to reverse):** require the full team; may escalate to the
  Chairman.
- **Strategy ties:** broken by The Principal.
- **Accessibility claims:** governed by the Accessibility Principal's hard veto.
- **Disagreement resolution:** disagree-and-commit on reversible calls; escalate
  to The Principal otherwise; authenticity/ethics disputes escalate to the
  Accessibility Principal's veto and then the Chairman.

## What escalates to the Chairman

Only legal, financial, ownership, or ethical approval — e.g. final company name +
trademark clearance, external spend or funding, equity/ownership structure, going
public with strategic material, and any case where authentic delivery conflicts
with a commercial incentive.

## Why

Speed on reversible decisions, care on irreversible ones, and an unbreakable
guarantee on the one thing that protects the whole company: authentic
accessibility. Full description on the [Team & operating model]({{ '/team/' | relative_url }}) page.
