## Decision

- Anchor on premium design engagements and standing retainers, where margin and
  stickiness live.
- Treat productized audits/ACRs as a **land** motion, always tied upward to design
  and recurring work — never sold as a price-shopped commodity.
- **Do not publish a rate card yet.** Validate willingness to pay with real
  proposals first.

## Why

Public pricing for commodity accessibility work trends to a documented
$100–$250/page race to the bottom. Public pricing for *premium* AI design and
accessibility retainers is thin — competitors quote custom. Inventing a rate card
we cannot defend would be both a commercial mistake and an authenticity violation
([DR-0002]({{ '/decisions/authenticity-guardrails/' | relative_url }})).

## Consequences

- Slower to present concrete prices publicly; intentional.
- Creates a research dependency: gather competitor proposals / RFP award data
  before formalizing pricing.
