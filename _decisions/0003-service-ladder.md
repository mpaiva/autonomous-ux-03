## Decision

A deliberate ladder (full detail on the [Services]({{ '/services/' | relative_url }})
page):

- **Productized (land):** AI Interface Accessibility Review (signature), scoped
  WCAG 2.2 AA audit, VPAT/ACR generation, remediation sprint, EU market-entry
  package, design-system hardening.
- **Core (flagship + margin):** premium AI/agentic product design, inclusive by
  default.
- **Recurring (the annuity):** embedded per-sprint accessibility design reviews,
  per-release VPAT/ACR refresh, monitoring/governance, design-system maintenance,
  startup enablement.

## Why

- Recurring revenue compounds *after* the one-time audit. Critically, an ACR is
  only valid for the version it tested — so **demand regenerates on the client's
  own ship cadence** without new selling.
- Productized offers are easy to buy and produce the finding that justifies the
  premium work.
- Embedded design reviews (shift-left) are the highest-margin, stickiest touchpoint
  and the natural fit for a *design* studio — cheaper for the client than post-code
  remediation.

## Trade-offs

- Productized audits risk pulling us toward commodity perception; we mitigate by
  always tying them upward to design and retainers (see
  [DR-0005]({{ '/decisions/pricing-posture/' | relative_url }})).
