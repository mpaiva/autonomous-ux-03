## Bottom line

The case for Accessible AI UX rests on **demand signals and willingness to pay**,
not on a total-addressable-market number. We will not put a hero TAM in any deck —
the market-size evidence is report-mill sourced and disagrees by multiples.

## Market size — treat as directional only (Low confidence)

- Digital accessibility software/services estimates range wildly: ~$0.8B→$1.08B
  (Mordor) vs ~$4.97B→$12.71B (Market Research Future); services ~$2B at ~15% CAGR
  (DataInsights). UX services CAGR quoted at both 5.1% and 31.2%.
- **No discrete "AI UX services" market-size figure exists** in citable form.

Sources: Mordor Intelligence, Market Research Future, DataInsights, Fortune
Business Insights / 360iResearch (all 2025–26). **So what:** use proxies, not TAM.

## Demand signals — the strongest evidence (High / Med-High)

- **EU Accessibility Act in force since 28 June 2025**; binds private-sector
  digital services on the EU market, including non-EU sellers (European
  Commission / AccessibleEU). Existing services have until 2030.
- **US HHS §504: WCAG 2.1 AA deadline held at 11 May 2026 — no extension.** Most
  urgent near-term US driver (Jackson Lewis, 2026).
- **ADA Title II** WCAG 2.1 AA deadlines for state/local gov **extended in April
  2026 to 2027/2028** — requirement locked, timing slipped (Federal Register).
- **Web-accessibility litigation: 3,117 federal suits in 2025, +27% YoY**,
  spreading into healthcare, finance, hospitality (Seyfarth Shaw, via Equidox).
- **Design hiring up ~60% in 2025**, with demand for designers of "conversational
  / generative AI" and agent behavior (ADPList State of Design 2025; Built In).
- **NN/g (2026):** surface UI is commoditizing; "trust will be a major design
  problem" for AI — and accessibility is **not mentioned**, confirming the gap.

## Willingness to pay (High where vendor-listed)

- Audits: $100–$250/page; mid-site projects ~$5k–$25k; enterprise $75k+.
- VPAT/ACR: $350–$650 add-on; $2k–$10k full engagements.
- Full SaaS compliance cycle: ~$30k–$60k.
- **Retainer / premium design day-rate pricing is thinly documented** — gather via
  real proposals before building a rate card (Low confidence).

## AI-interface pain points — the craft wedge (Med / Med-High)

- Screen-reader users cannot easily navigate generated chat output; must read the
  whole page top-down or hunt per-message copy buttons (Harvard URC, 2024–25).
- Streaming/generative output breaks WCAG focus-management and status-message
  expectations; live regions are an unsolved pattern (TestParty, 2025).
- Voice-only features exclude users absent text parity (TestParty, 2025).

**So what for positioning:** lead with risk + deadline + craft. The defensible
niche is accessible patterns for generative/streaming/agentic/voice interfaces —
exactly what commodity auditors and overlays do not solve.

## Honest gaps

Market-size totals are Low confidence; "AI UX market" has no citable figure;
premium and retainer pricing need primary validation; the litigation figure traces
to Seyfarth via secondary blogs (confirm against the primary report before external
use).
